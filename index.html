Install on Your iPhone

Momentum works best as an app. To install:

  1. Tap the Share button ⎋ at the bottom of Safari
  2. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  3. Tap "Add" — done!

Launches full screen, works offline, just like a native app.
